How to Play

Controls

  • → Right Arrow: Move right
  • ← Left Arrow: Move left
  • Space Bar: Jump

Objective

Guide your cat through challenging platforms and obstacles. Collect coins to increase your score and avoid enemies. Reach the end of each level to progress!

Tips

  • Watch out for spikes and enemies!
  • Time your jumps carefully
  • Collect coins for extra points
  • You have 3 lives - use them wisely!
